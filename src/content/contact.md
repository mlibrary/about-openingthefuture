If you have any questions or would like to sign up please email us on xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx to discuss any aspect of the model, pricing, what's included etc.

For sign ups please include the following details to make the process quick and easy for your library:

1. Contact name
2. Name and address of institution
3. VAT number if applicable
4. What Band/Tier your institution is in
5. Which package(s) of backlist books or OA Supporter Membership you've chosen
6. Institutional IP address range and/or OpenAthens ID so we can set up your access to the backlist books 
7. If you'd like to pay annually or all upfront in one payment
8. If you give permission for your institution's name to be added to our list of member supporters on our webpage, and for us to make a short news announcement on here and on social media

We'll pass those details onto the appropriate invoicing partner for you and will then delete the email - we don't store this data in our inbox.

And if you're not yet sure about signing up, we're always happy to schedule a Zoom call with you if it would help, and we have lots of extra information we can send (links to useful articles, reports, previous webinars we've given, and lot's more).
