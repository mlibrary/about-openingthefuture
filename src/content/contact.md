## For Publishers

If you would like to discuss how OtF might work at your own press, reach out to the publishing and technology experts at Fulcrum via email at **[openingthefuture@umich.edu](mailto:openingthefuture@umich.edu)** 


## For Libraries

If your library is interested in enhancing your local collection through a subscription to Opening the Future please email Lyrasis on **membersupport@lyrasis.org** to discuss any aspect of the program, pricing, and which book package(s) you'd like to access.

Please include the following details to make the process quick and easy for your library:

* Contact name.
* Name and address of institution.
* VAT number if applicable.
* What Band/Tier your institution is in. Library and institutional members are tiered. Banding for US Institutions is tiered based on the [Basic Classification from Carnegie Classification](https://carnegieclassifications.iu.edu/classification_descriptions/basic.php). In Canada we use the [CRKN banding system](https://www.crkn-rcdr.ca/en/banding-system). If your institution is based in a country covered by the [Research4Life program](https://www.research4life.org/access/institutions-registered/), please use the smallest tier pricing.
* Which package(s) of backlist books or the OA Supporter Membership you've chosen.
* Institutional IP address range and/or OpenAthens ID so we can set up your access to the backlist books.
* If you'd like to pay annually or all upfront in one payment.
* If you give permission for your institution's name to be added to our list of member supporters on our webpage, and for us to make a short news announcement on here and on social media.
